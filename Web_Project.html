Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • pea
  • Masala

Recipe

  1. Take Paneer and pea
  2. make tomato gravy
  3. Deep fry the paneer
  4. Pour paneer and pea in the gravy and garnish it with coriander leaves
  5. Ready to serve.

Samosa

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Potato
  • Masala

Recipe

  1. Take Maida Paste
  2. Put smashed potato on it
  3. mix masala well before puting it in the maida paste
  4. Deep fry it.
  5. Ready to serve.
